About Me

I am an American singer/songwriter and instrumentalist. I am signed to Warner Music Nashville, have been since high school. I have been performing and writing music every since the age of four, and that passion has only grown since then. I am passionate about my music, all music really and I enjoy learning how to play different instruments. One of the hardest instruments I had to learn was the pedal guitar, but I thoroughly enjoyed the challenge.
